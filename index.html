About

Geoscientist &
Field Researcher

Bridging geological theory with hands-on fieldwork across India's most fascinating tectonic and impact settings.

I am a PhD researcher at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, specializing in impact geology, sedimentary basin analysis, and structural geoscience. My academic journey spans IIT Kharagpur (M.Sc, 9.18/10) and Durgapur Government College (B.Sc).

My research centers on the Lonar Impact Crater in Maharashtra — one of the world's best-preserved meteorite impact structures. I have led multiple field expeditions, investigating impact direction through microfracture density and XRD analysis and generating high-resolution DEMs for ejecta characterization.

I also work on sedimentary stylolite roughness inversion to quantify burial and erosion histories, collaborating internationally with researchers from France and Germany.

Beyond research, I serve as a Teaching Assistant at IIT Kanpur. I hold a 1st Division from Bharat Kala Kendra (5th year, painting) and follow cricket closely.

Research & Field

Projects &
Expeditions

Field-driven research spanning impact craters, sedimentary basins, and structural geology across India.

PhD Field Work
Lonar Impact Crater Expeditions
Maharashtra, India · 2 Expeditions
Led a 9-day expedition mapping impact ejecta distribution using QGIS for detailed debris analysis. Led a separate 5-day expedition generating a high-resolution DEM of the crater. Research contributed to peer-reviewed publications on impact direction reconstruction and double-layered ejecta morphology.
M.Sc Thesis
Critical Taper Model: Concept and Applications
IIT Kharagpur · Supervised by Prof. Saibal Gupta
In-depth investigation of the critical taper model applied to fold-and-thrust belts, exploring mathematical foundations and geological applications to accretionary wedge dynamics and crustal deformation.
M.Sc Fieldwork
Dec 2019
Sedimentary & Structural Mapping
Beohari (MP) · Angul (Odisha) · India
Fieldwork identifying primary sedimentary structures, paleocurrent directions, lithology and sand body architecture in Beohari. Separate campaign for regional outcrop mapping and strain analysis in Angul District, Odisha.
